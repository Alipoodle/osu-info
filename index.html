osu!info

A Windows program that can get available osu! information with a UI using some elements of Material Design

Download

Accounts

Enter the name of any player to get their stats, top and recent scores all in seperate tabs.

Top Scores

View a players top scores and the rank they got. You can also click on the map to open a link to download it.

Recent

View a players recent plays, their scores and weather they failed or not.

Customise

Choose between light and dark themes and also select a custom theme colour.
